# The Living Blackboard Classroom

RuBrik presentation theme: a giant dark-green classroom blackboard with wooden frame, chalk tray, colored chalk, eraser, sticky-note teacher comments, pinned paper cards, rough chalk outlines, live handwriting, dust, classroom-bell transitions, pop quizzes, achievements, and playful office-safe jokes.

Design principles:
- The board must feel alive, hand-drawn, and imperfect.
- Use colorful chalk accents: yellow, pink, blue, green, red, and white.
- Explain product concepts like a gifted teacher drawing diagrams on a blackboard.
- Prefer dashed borders, sketchy boxes, chalk texture, stamps, circles, underlines, arrows, formulas, and classroom gimmicks over corporate cards.
- Keep text large for 1366×768 and 1920×1080 screens.
- Respect reduced motion by disabling long animations and dust-heavy effects.
